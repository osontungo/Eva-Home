About Us


Eva

was created by a 13 year old as a way to help decentralize social media and provide a way for creators to earn for the content they own and create. All data stored on Eva is stored on the DeSo blockchain (Decentralized social blockchain). Eva allows for native blockchain features such as converting your messages into NFTs and allowing users to invest in creators they believe. Eva is completely open source, free, add-less, and distributed. Make sure to check out the Eva Twitter page or join the discord server. A new revolution in social media is coming!
